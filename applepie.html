Apple Pie

Price: $12.95

Description

Bethany received the recipe for this delicious pie from her grandmother. At the age of 5, she was already baking this all-American traditional apple pie at her home. As the recipe goes a long way back, it contains the most typical ingredients used for so many years to make this classic and popular pie.

This pie is available every dayand comes in 2 sizes. You can serve it cold or warm it for 4 minutes in the microwave to give it an extra touch. Your house will smell just like Bethany's Shop.

This recipe has been in our family for generations. We hope you enjoy this pie as much as we do - Bethany

How we make this pie:

  1. First, we combine sugar, flour and spices. We toss the apples with some lemon juice and add sugar.
  2. We then creaate the bottom crust and fill that with apple mixture. We put some butter on that.We then roll remaining crust to fit top of pie.
  3. Then we beat the eggs until we have a foamy mix. That is then brushed over the crust. We sprinkle some extra sugar.
  4. Then the pie goes into the oven at 360o for 35 minutes. When ready, we let it cool on a wire rack.

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Cinnamon
  • Ginger
  • Nutmeg
  • Apples
  • Lemons
